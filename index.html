WELCOME TO ZOMATO

Zomato

        Zomato is an Indian multinational restaurant aggregator and food delivery company.
        It was founded by Deepinder Goyal andPankaj Chaddah  in 2008.Zomato provides 
        information,menus and user_reviews of restaurants as well as food delivery options
        from partner restaurants in more than 1000 Indian cities and towns,as of 2022-2023.
        Zomato rivals Swiggy in food delivery and hyperlocal space.
